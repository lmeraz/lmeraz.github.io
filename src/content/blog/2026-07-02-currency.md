I was recently asked what my currency and cons are to the acting industry. It's a nice way to asses the peculiar differentiators that set one apart from the sea of actors. I'll be humble, I have a lot of potential, but I recognize my shortcomings and am working to minimize them.

## Currency
- Financial freedom
- Ambition
- Passion
- Willingness to open doors
- Intentional theatrical training from top teachers
- Single
- Growing network of artists
- Non-union status
- Integrity for the craft
- Technological prowess
- Connections to benefactors in other industries
- Location agnostic

## Cons
- No professional acting credits or experience
- No musical training
- No conservatory training
- Few relationships with producers/writers/directors in industry
- Challenging immigration situation for international work
