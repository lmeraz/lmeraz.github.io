About Me

I’m Luis, a bilingual Mexican American actor passionate about storytelling. I used to code at Meta and Lyft. I now inspire people to lead with courage, love, and joy through delight in my craft. I act, improvise, clown, and write. My production company, Do Tell, produces theater and shorts with rave reviews. Beyond entertainment, I surf, sail, DJ, and hike with my dog, Manny.
