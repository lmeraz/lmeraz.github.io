How to study:
Memory Stages
Attention, Encoding, Retrieval

Optminzing Encoding: Use GOAT
Generate Test
Quiz self, teach others, think out loud, mimick, test

Organize
Reduce load on brain by coloring, position, allocating, material, outline, color, picture, reslated, chunk

Avoid Illusions of learning
Two kinds of memory: recognition & recall
Recognition - when you can't think of it on own
Recall - remember information on own, avoid methods of recognition, need recall.

Take breaks
use short session study do something else, repeat. You also need sleep! Take naps. Study over course of days not ours.

Match learning and testing conditions
All conditions for retrieval of info should be the same as those during studying study under many differnet condtions. So information comes easily regardless.

Elaborate
Make associations with the material. How does it all fit together. Draw, doodle, visualize, think.

Most Effective Techniques
Practice testing - Flashcards, questions tests.
Distributed PRactice - study in short sessions, test after sleep

Moderately Effective
Elaborative Interrogation - Use why questions
Self Explanation - Provide own explanations when loearning
Interleaved Practice - Mix different kinds of problems.

Least Effective
Highlighting / Underlining
Rereading
summarization
menomnis - key words
imagery of learning
