title: Questions to ask yourself when studying
---
Often you need to take time out of your schedule really understand something deeply. Below are the questions and practices to consider when undertaking a new study habit.

### Questions to ask before you start
- What are you trying to do?
- What do you want to achieve?
- What information do you have?

### Tools to help as you study
- Mnemonics
- Mind Palace
- Hardwire problem-solving
- Back away from a problem
- Exercise helps neurons survive
- Tune out people undercut
- Value of teamwork
- The first principle is that you must not fool yourself.

### Questions to ask after your study session
- Did you make a serious effort to understand the text?
- Did you work with classmates?
- Did you attempt to outline every homework problem solution?
- Did you participate actively in homework groups?
- Did you consult with the instructor?
- Did you understand all of your homework problem solutions?
- Did you ask in class for explanations?
- Did you have a study guide?
- Did you outline the homework solutions quickly?
- Did you go over the study guide problems with classmates and quiz one another?
- Did you go to review?
- Did you sleep?
